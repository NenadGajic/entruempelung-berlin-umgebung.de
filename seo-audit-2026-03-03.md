# SEO Audit: entruempelung-berlin-umgebung.de

**Audit date:** 2026-03-03

## Executive Summary

The site has solid SEO foundations: proper canonical tags, robots.txt, sitemap, structured data (JSON-LD), Open Graph/Twitter cards, and no indexation blockers. The main issues are **title tag redundancy/length** and **thin heading hierarchy on service pages**. Below are findings ordered by impact.

---

## Critical Issues

### 1. Duplicate title tag on `/entruempelung` — High Impact

**Issue:** The title resolves to *"Entrümpelung in Berlin und Umgebung | Entrümpelung in Berlin und Umgebung"* — both halves are identical.

**Evidence:** `services.php:5` sets `meta_title` to the same phrase as the global `$page->title` in `config.php:9`.

**Fix:** Change `meta_title` in `services.php` to something differentiated, e.g.:
- `"Professionelle Entrümpelung – Wohnungen, Häuser, Keller & mehr"`

---

## High-Impact Issues

### 2. Homepage title too long (~79 chars) — High

**Issue:** `"Entrümpelung und Entsorgung in Berlin | Entrümpelung in Berlin und Umgebung"` will be truncated in SERPs at ~60 chars.

**Fix:** Shorten the site-wide brand suffix or use a shorter variant for the homepage. For example:
- `"Entrümpelung & Entsorgung in Berlin und Umgebung | Schnell & Zuverlässig"`

### 3. Service page titles are long (65-79 chars) — Medium-High

**Issue:** The pattern `{service meta_title} | Entrümpelung in Berlin und Umgebung` produces titles that are 62-79 characters. The brand suffix alone is 38 chars.

**Fix:** Shorten the brand suffix for inner pages. Options:
- Use `"| Entrümpelung Berlin & Umgebung"` (33 chars) instead of the full name
- Or set a separate `titleSuffix` config value for inner pages

### 4. H1 tags on service pages are too short — Medium-High

**Issue:** Service page H1s are single generic words: "Entrümpelung", "Entsorgung", "Umzug", etc. They lack geographic modifiers ("Berlin und Umgebung") and qualifying terms.

**Evidence:** `service-page.blade.php:12` renders `$service['h1']` directly; `services.php` defines these as bare nouns.

**Fix:** Update `h1` values in `services.php` to include the full service area:

| Current | Suggested |
|---------|-----------|
| `Entrümpelung` | `Entrümpelung in Berlin und Umgebung` |
| `Entsorgung` | `Entsorgung in Berlin und Umgebung` |
| `Haushalts- und Gewerbeauflösung` | `Haushalts- und Gewerbeauflösung in Berlin und Umgebung` |
| `Umzug` | `Umzug in Berlin und Umgebung` |
| `Transport` | `Kleintransporte in Berlin und Umgebung` |

### 5. Heading hierarchy gap: H1 jumps to H3 — Medium

**Issue:** On all service pages, the H1 is in the breadcrumb hero, then the next heading is `<h3>` (`service-page.blade.php:51`), skipping H2 entirely. H4 follows for detail sections. This is a heading-level violation.

**Fix:** Change `<h3>` on line 51 of `service-page.blade.php` to `<h2>`, and `<h4>` tags to `<h3>`.

---

## Medium-Impact Issues

### 6. `/anfrage` meta description too long (~190 chars) — Medium

**Issue:** Google truncates at ~155-160 chars. The end ("...Umzug oder Transport.") will be cut.

**Fix:** Trim to ~155 chars:
> `"Kostenlose Anfrage für Entrümpelung, Entsorgung, Auflösung, Umzug oder Transport in Berlin und Umgebung – transparentes Angebot inklusive."`

### 7. Static `keywords` meta tag on all pages — Low-Medium

**Issue:** `main.blade.php:16` has a hardcoded `<meta name="keywords">` tag. Google has officially ignored this tag since 2009. It doesn't help rankings and reveals your target keywords to competitors.

**Fix:** Remove the `<meta name="keywords">` line from `main.blade.php`.

### 8. No `Service` schema on service pages — Medium

**Issue:** Service pages only inherit the global WebSite + LocalBusiness schemas. Adding `Service` schema (with `provider`, `areaServed`, `description`) would enhance rich result eligibility.

**Fix:** Add per-service JSON-LD `Service` blocks to the structured data component when on a service page.

---

## Low-Impact / Advisory

### 9. Inconsistent CTA link patterns on homepage

Some CTAs use `{{ $page->baseUrl }}/anfrage`, others use bare `/anfrage`. Both work, but the inconsistency is messy. Standardize to bare `/anfrage` per the CLAUDE.md editing rule ("Keep internal links as absolute site paths").

### 10. Sitemap `changefreq: daily` is inaccurate

A static marketing site doesn't change daily. Google largely ignores this, but `weekly` or `monthly` would be more honest. Low priority.

### 11. No `hreflang` tag

The site is German-only (`de_DE`), so `hreflang` isn't strictly needed. If you ever add multilingual support, this would be required.

### 12. No `<link rel="preload">` for critical assets

Hero images and fonts could benefit from preloading hints to improve LCP. Consider adding `<link rel="preload">` for the primary hero image and font files.

---

## What's Working Well

- **Canonical tags** present and correct on all pages
- **robots.txt** clean, allows all crawlers, references sitemap
- **Sitemap** includes all 7 pages, no phantom URLs
- **Structured data**: WebSite + LocalBusiness + BreadcrumbList (on inner pages)
- **Open Graph + Twitter Card** meta tags on every page
- **`robots: index, follow`** explicitly set
- **No orphan pages** — all pages reachable from nav + footer
- **Image alt texts** present on all content images (decorative icons correctly use `alt="" aria-hidden="true"`)
- **URL structure** is clean, descriptive, lowercase, hyphen-separated
- **HTTPS** with correct canonical URLs
- **Mobile viewport** configured
